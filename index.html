Diseases and Their Remedies

A quick guide to managing common ailments

Common Diseases

  • Cold and Flu

    Drink plenty of fluids, rest, and use over-the-counter medications for relief.

  • Headache

    Stay hydrated, rest in a quiet dark room, and take pain relievers if necessary.

  • Acidity

    Avoid spicy foods, eat small meals, and drink warm water after meals.

  • Diabetes

    Maintain a balanced diet, exercise regularly, and monitor blood sugar levels.

  • Hypertension

    Reduce salt intake, practice stress management, and follow prescribed medication.

  • Constipation

    Increase fiber intake, drink plenty of water, and stay physically active.

  • Asthma

    Avoid triggers, use prescribed inhalers, and practice breathing exercises.

  • Allergies

    Identify and avoid allergens, use antihistamines, and consult a doctor if severe.

  • Back Pain

    Maintain good posture, do stretching exercises, and use hot or cold packs for relief.

  • Anemia

    Consume iron-rich foods like spinach, red meat, and legumes; take supplements if needed.
